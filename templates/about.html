Once you select and submit a state name, you will receive a set of tables and graphs. The first row of graphs represent the trends in average payout of malpractice cases, annual number of cases per one million residents, and the total amount spent on malpractice case payouts by practitioners and institutions in this state over the last 27 years. The second row of graphs show you our random forest analysis of how important a variety of metrics are in determining the cost and prevalence of malpractice cases. Finally, the third row of tables provides a ranking of all states in this study for the three measures mentioned, which is summarized for your specific state at the bottom of the page.
